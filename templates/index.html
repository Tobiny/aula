About Smart Classroom

Smart Classroom is an innovative system that uses emotion recognition technology to help teachers understand their students' engagement and emotional responses during class. The system:

  • Captures real-time emotion data through webcam analysis
  • Provides insights into student emotional states during lessons
  • Helps identify moments of high engagement or confusion
  • Allows teachers to adapt their teaching methods based on feedback

Note: All emotion data is processed locally and is only used to provide feedback to the teacher. No personal data is stored or shared.
